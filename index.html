Our Mission

Engineering Voices highlights the experiences of underrepresented engineers to inspire the next generation and create more inclusive workplaces.

Through authentic storytelling and meaningful conversations, we're building bridges between diverse engineering communities and amplifying voices that have been historically underrepresented in the field.
